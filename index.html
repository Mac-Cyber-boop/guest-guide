Day 1: Arrival & Reaching New Heights

Schedule & Plan

  • 10:35 AMDepart from Delhi (DEL) on Emirates.
  • 01:00 PMArrive at Dubai (DXB).
  • 02:00 PMTake a taxi to Holiday Inn Express Safa Park.
  • 03:00 PMCheck-in and relax.
  • 05:30 PMTaxi to Dubai Mall.
  • 06:00 PMExplore the mall, see the fountains.
  • 07:30 PMBurj Khalifa (Levels 124 & 125) entry.
  • 09:00 PMDinner at a restaurant in/near Dubai Mall.

Cost Breakdown (for 2)

  • Cab (Airport to Hotel): ₹1,135
  • Cab (Hotel to Dubai Mall & back): ₹680
  • Burj Khalifa Tickets: ₹7,458
  • Dinner (Mid-range): ₹3,640

Day 2: Sky Views & Desert Adventure

Schedule & Plan

  • 09:00 AMBreakfast at the hotel.
  • 11:00 AMTaxi to Sky Views Observatory.
  • 11:30 AMEnjoy the views and glass slide.
  • 01:30 PMLunch at a food court/cafe.
  • 03:00 PMPickup from hotel for Desert Safari.
  • 04:00 PMDune bashing, quad biking, camel rides.
  • 07:00 PMBBQ Dinner and entertainment at Al Khayma Camp.
  • 09:30 PMDrop-off back at the hotel.

Cost Breakdown (for 2)

  • Cab (Hotel to Sky Views): ₹340
  • Sky Views Tickets: ₹7,458
  • Desert Safari Tour: ₹6,686
  • Lunch (Food Court): ₹1,820

Note: Desert Safari includes hotel pickup/drop-off and dinner, so no extra transport or dinner costs are needed for the evening.
